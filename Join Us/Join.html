How to sign up for tutoring:

1. The parent fills out tutoring form for each child (elementary or middle), giving us basic academic and personal information to provide a dynamic, engaging learning experience tailored specifically for each child.

2. Our team of committed tutors will contact the parent, confirming their child's information and putting in them into contact with their assigned tutor.

3. One of our enthusiastic, high-school tutors will contact you immediately to schedule the first tutoring session.
